Mission Statement

The main objective of the African American Club is to enhance student knowledge of African American culture, society, history and background. The African American Club also allows students the opportunity to discuss important issues in African American society and their lives and how to educate themselves about the world around them. The African American Club is open to all students and meets regularly throughout the school year.
