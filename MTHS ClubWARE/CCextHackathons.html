HackPHS 2019 | HackNJIT 2019 | Hack TCNJ 2019 | HackRU 2018

HackRU 2018 was a revolutionary Hackathon for the Computer Club. It was the first time it's members attempted to make malware of any sort, something that would symbolize the Hardware Strand for the rest of its time. While confronted with multiple issues regarding feigning icons, the trojan looked like an iTunes installer and prevented bootup from installing shutdown commands into multiple startup folders, each acting as a contigency. Only a 12 hour hackathon, it displayed the birth of a new era, following the dark ages of a club reduced to no innovation and only video games.
