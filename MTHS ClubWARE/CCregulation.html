Become a regulator!

The IT strand of the Computer Club is the sole provider and regulator of the information on the MTHS ClubWARE website. The executive members of the IT strand have worked hard on creating the beta (1.0) version of the clubWARE. Currently, there are 60 clubs registured within MTHS. In order to remain in contact with clubs who are clients of the MTHS ClubWARE, we ask for indivdiuals who participate in such clubs to participate as Webpage Regulators. Regular members of the IT strand, and even those who do not wish to join the Computer Club, can participate as a webpage regulator. Webpage Regulators are in charge of recieving the commands and requests of their respective club, and coordinating with the IT strand in order to fulfill these requests.


Criteria

A potential Website Regulator must attend 75% of the meetings of their respective club. It is not necessary for the regulator to attend Computer Club meetings, unless they are given information from the club client in regards to their page. Once chosen and participated in the respective duties, Website Regulators will have the opportunity to have their name, and club (for whom they are a website regulator) placed in the "Collaborators" page of the ClubWARE website.


Contact Us

Please contact the current collaborators via the Collaborators Page or use the Support email for more information about the process.
