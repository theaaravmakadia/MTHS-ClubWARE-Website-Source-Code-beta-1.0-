Mission Statement

Mathletes is a competitive club where you can showcase your advanced and creative mathematical skills. Mathletes questions make you think critically, exposing you to new facets of math that you would have never even imagined. Once the hour of brain power of your 10 questions is over, you get to enjoy complimentary pizza and drinks while waiting for your results. This engaging club brings friends and intriguing math together, a combination most definitely worth experiencing. Don’t wait, and join our more than 80 members now!
