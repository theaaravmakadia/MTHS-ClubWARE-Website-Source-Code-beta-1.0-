Mission Statement

The MTHS Robotics Club seeks to tackle the "STEM" problem of our generation by immersing students in an environment focused on the engineering process. The club caters to every student's individual need, with the educational track for new students, and the VEX EDR competition experience for more knowledgeable members. The teams are completely student-run, allowing members to truly rise to the challenge and demonstrate their potential.
